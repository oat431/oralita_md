---

## Session Protocol

1. **Warm-up (5-8 min):** 5 min treadmill walk or cycle + dynamic stretches (leg swings, arm circles, cat-cow, bodyweight squat × 10)
2. **Compounds first.** Then accessories. Rest 2 min between compound sets, 60-90 sec on accessories.
3. **Log everything.** Write down weight × reps after every set. No log = no adjustments next session.
4. **Cool-down:** 5 min walk + stretch tight areas.

---

## Progression Rules

- ✅ Complete all reps with good form → add weight next session
- ⚠️ Miss 2+ reps on a lift → repeat same weight next session
- ❌ Miss reps two sessions in a row → drop 10% and build back up
- 🛑 Sharp pain (not muscle burn) → stop the exercise, tell me

---

## Recovery Checklist

- [ ] 6+ hours sleep (protect this)
- [ ] 2,400 kcal with 160g+ protein
- [ ] 2-3L water daily
- [ ] If soreness prevents full ROM → active recovery (walk, light cycle), don't force the lift
