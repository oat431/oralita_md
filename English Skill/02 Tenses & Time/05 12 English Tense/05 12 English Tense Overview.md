# 12 English Tenses — Overview

English expresses time through 12 combinations: 3 times × 4 aspects. Use this as your index — each tense has its own deep-dive file.

---

## The Master Grid

| Form \ Time | **Past** | **Present** | **Future** |
|-------------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Simple** | S + V2 | S + V1(s/es) | S + will + V1 |
| **Continuous** | S + was/were + Ving | S + am/is/are + Ving | S + will + be + Ving |
| **Perfect** | S + had + V3 | S + has/have + V3 | S + will + have + V3 |
| **Perfect Continuous** | S + had + been + Ving | S + has/have + been + Ving | S + will + have + been + Ving |

### Symbols
| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| S | Subject |
| V1 | Base form (go, eat, see) |
| V2 | Past form (went, ate, saw) |
| V3 | Past Participle (gone, eaten, seen) |
| Ving | Present Participle (going, eating, seeing) |

## Decision Flow

```
Is the action...
├── In the PAST?
│   ├── Simple completed? → 05.5 Past Simple
│   ├── In progress at a past moment? → 05.6 Past Continuous
│   ├── Before another past action? → 05.7 Past Perfect
│   └── Duration before another past? → 05.8 Past Perfect Continuous
│
├── In the PRESENT?
│   ├── Fact / habit / routine? → 05.1 Present Simple
│   ├── Happening NOW? → 05.2 Present Continuous
│   ├── Past → present connection? → 05.3 Present Perfect
│   └── Duration from past → now? → 05.4 Present Perfect Continuous
│
└── In the FUTURE?
    ├── Prediction / spontaneous? → 05.9 Future Simple
    ├── In progress at future time? → 05.10 Future Continuous
    ├── Completed before future time? → 05.11 Future Perfect
    └── Duration up to future point? → 05.12 Future Perfect Continuous
```
