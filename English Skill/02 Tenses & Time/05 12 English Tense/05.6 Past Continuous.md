# 05.6 Past Continuous

> **Formula:** S + was/were + Ving

The "interrupted action" tense — for actions in progress at a specific past moment, often interrupted by another event.

---

## Structure

| | Affirmative | Negative | Question |
|---|------------|----------|----------|
| I / He / She / It | I **was working**. | I **wasn't working**. | **Was** she working? |
| You / We / They | They **were working**. | They **weren't working**. | **Were** they working? |

---

## When to Use

### 1. Interrupted Action (Past Continuous + Past Simple)
An action in progress (Past Continuous) is interrupted by another action (Past Simple).

| Example | Pattern |
|---------|---------|
| I **was sleeping** when the phone **rang**. | Longer action + interruption |
| She **was driving** home when she **saw** the accident. | Background + event |
| When I **arrived**, they **were having** dinner. | Event + background |
| He **was taking** a shower when the power **went out**. | In progress + sudden event |

> **When** introduces the interruption (Past Simple). **While** introduces the ongoing action (Past Continuous).
> "**When** I called, she was cooking." / "**While** she was cooking, I called."

### 2. Specific Past Moment
What was happening at an exact time in the past.

| Example | Time |
|---------|------|
| At 8pm last night, I **was watching** TV. | Exact past moment |
| This time yesterday, I **was sitting** on the beach. | Specific past time |
| What **were** you **doing** at 3am? | Asking about a specific past moment |

### 3. Simultaneous Past Actions (Both in Progress)
Two actions happening at the same time in the past.

| Example |
|---------|
| I **was cooking** while she **was setting** the table. |
| They **were talking** and the children **were playing** outside. |

### 4. Background / Atmosphere (Storytelling)
Setting the scene in a story or narrative.

| Example |
|---------|
| The sun **was shining**, birds **were singing**, and people **were walking** in the park. Suddenly... |
| It **was raining** heavily. The wind **was blowing**. |

---

## Past Continuous vs Past Simple

| Past Continuous | Past Simple |
|----------------|-------------|
| Action in progress (background) | Completed action (foreground) |
| I **was eating** dinner. | I **ate** dinner. |
| Focus on the process | Focus on completion |
| Often interrupted | Often the interruption |

---

## ⚠️ Thai Speaker Traps

| ❌ Error | ✅ Correct | Why |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| I was sleep when you called. | I **was sleeping** when you called. | Missing -ing |
| When I came, she cooked. | When I came, she **was cooking**. | Simple past = she started after I arrived; continuous = already in progress |
| He sleeping at 10pm. | He **was sleeping** at 10pm. | Missing "was" |
| They was playing. | They **were** playing. | "Were" for plural |

---

## Quick Test

1. I ___ (watch) TV when she called. → **was watching**
2. At midnight, they ___ (still/dance). → **were still dancing**
3. She ___ (not/listen) while I was talking. → **wasn't listening**
4. ___ you ___ (sleep) at 2am? → **Were / sleeping**
5. The sun ___ (shine) and the birds ___ (sing). → **was shining / were singing**
